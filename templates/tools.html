Magical Tools

The best magical tools are ones which you make or find in the wild. When choosing tools, select items which feel personal and appealing to you. Cauldron

Cauldrons symbolize the element of water, but they are also used to contain fire. Cauldrons appear in ancient lore as fonts of wisdom and sources of sustenance. They retain enormous magical power today.

Candles are one of the most important tools, and have multiple uses in both rituals and magic. Candles symbolize the element of fire, but can also be associated with the directions, deities or any other number of things.

Sage

Sage has long been known as the herb for wisdom and is used to cleanse the area of unwanted energies. When burned, the sage creates a thick, grayish smoke. Sage...this is the old stand by. The Celts believed that if a bride carried sage, she became wise.
